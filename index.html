Our Features

Features we have implemented in our service that you can look forward to.

Set Smart Goals

Enable users to set Specific, Measurable, Achievable, Relevant, Time-Bound (S.M.A.R.T) Goals.

Recommended Meal Plan

About Us

What We Do. Why We Do. How We Do.

During Covid'19, we saw firsthand how countless of Singaporean's livelihoods and way of life are affected. Many turn to tutorials and online videos of workoiut routines and guides. However, these videos do not address the fact that everyone's body is different and reacts differently. Following the wrong guide or videos might cause harm instead.

What We Do

We provide a service that helps individuals achieve any goals - from weight loss to a healthier lifestyle

Why We Do

We aim to help our users adopt winning habits that have long lasting changes

How We Do

We go through rounds of brainstorming and coding trial and errors to create our web application and smart watch

Why We Do What We Do

After seeing how people exercise wrongly firsthand and not getting the results they want to achieve, we decided to start from basics where they get to follow healthier options and proper ways to achieve a healthier lifestyle

We came up with the idea of our Web Application and SmartWatch in hopes that it will aid our users on their journey to a healthier lifestyle

How We Do What We Do

Our team first started by coming together for a discussion, choosing what we would like to produce and create a service for

After thorough discussion and brainstorming, our group has decided to go for a Winning Habits theme where we provide our users a service to achieve any goals - from weight loss to a healthier lfiestyle

We created wireframes as a foundation to visualise what we want our final product to look like and the features they have that our users can look forward to when using our service and products
